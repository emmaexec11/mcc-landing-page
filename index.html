Frequently Asked Questions

Do I get to choose my shades?+
The Starter Set comes with our 3 best-sellers: Hazelnut, Fallen Leaves, and Raspberry Kiss. We picked these because they're universally flattering and the easiest way to find your perfect match without the guesswork.
What exactly is a Multi-Colour Cream?+
Think of it as your whole makeup bag in one tiny tin. It's a creamy, blendable balm you can use on your lips, cheeks, and eyes. Just dab it on with your fingers (or a brush if you're fancy) and you're done. Perfect for those "I have 5 minutes" mornings.
Are the ingredients really 100% natural?+
Yep! We're a bit obsessed with clean formulas. All our ingredients are rated safe by the EWG, and we use real fruit and plant pigments (like beetroot and bilberry) instead of synthetic dyes. No parabens, no harsh chemicals, no sketchy stuff.
Is it safe for sensitive or acne-prone skin?+
We made this with sensitive skin in mind. The formula is non-comedogenic (won't clog pores) and fragrance-free. That said, everyone's skin is different — so if you're extra reactive, do a quick patch test first just to be safe.
How long does the colour last?+
You can expect about 4-6 hours of wear, depending on your skin type. The formula has a subtle stain effect, so even as it fades, you'll still have a hint of colour. And the tins are small enough to toss in your bag for easy touch-ups.
Is it vegan and cruelty-free?+
100%. No animal ingredients, never tested on animals. We're pretty firm on that one.
What's the shipping & satisfaction policy?+
Free shipping is included with the Starter Set. And while we can't accept returns on opened natural products, we do have a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If something's off, just reach out — we'll make it right.
Can I use it around my eyes?+
Absolutely. The formula is made with food-grade and mineral-based ingredients, so it's gentle enough for the delicate eye area. A lot of our customers use it as a quick eyeshadow.
